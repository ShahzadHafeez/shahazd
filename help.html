What is my allowance for checked baggage?

Tiger Airline has one of the world's most generous baggage policies. Economy Class customers can check in up to 35kg depending on the fare type, Business Class passengers 40kg and First Class passengers up to 50kg. Passengers in all three classes can check in up to 10 pieces of baggage as long as it stays within the checked weight limit for their cabin class.
